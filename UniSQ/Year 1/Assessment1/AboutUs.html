About Us

We are a new department of the Australian Government. Our goal is for e-transportation to be successful in all of its forms. We are here to provide you with information about various alternative e-vehicles. We have been in operation since August 2023. Our goal is to increase awareness around e-vehicles and connect our residents to alternative commuting options that will reduce the carbon footprint of our nation as a whole.
